Who We Are

About Unity Power Solutions

We are the leading system integrators for power infrastructure & backup solutions. Established in 2018, Unity Power Solutions offers comprehensive power solutions and services to all types of industries, enterprises, and corporates.

Our solutions encompass UPS systems with batteries (VRLA, SMF and Fibler battery), Battery Monitoring Systems, Servo Stabilizers, Active and Passive Filters, Automatic Transfer Switches & Inverters.
